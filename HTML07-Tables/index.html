Which stack is the most in demand?

The most in demand stack depends on the location you're in. Generally startups, mid-sized companies tend to use MERN stack. While that's true,enterprise companies and banks tend to use Spring boot technologies because of the high security and robustness.

How much time it takes to learn full-stack development?

It's up to how much time you put in effort to learn backend and frontend technologies, but generally it takes at least 2 years to really master the intricacies of full stack development!
